When will registration be available?

Online registration for the 2016 Northwest Summer Music Camp is up and running!

Do I need to pay the full camp fee all at one time?

NO! You may pay a deposit, and make multiple payments towards your camp balance. Once you begin the online registration process, you will see our payment options. OR...you may pay the entire amount during your online registration.

What is the deadline to apply for Summer Music Camp?

While there is not deadline for application, we encourage you to apply as soon as possible, especially if you play a popular instrument such as flute, trumpet, or saxophone.

Do I need to send an audition CD or tape?

No.

Will I need to audition for ensemble placement when I arrive for Camp?

How is my enrollment into camp determined?

Students are accepted into Summer Music Camp on a first come, first served basis.

What happens if there isn't room for me in Summer Music Camp by the time my application is processed?

You will be placed on our waiting list, and your payment will be refunded entirely if we are unable to include you for this summer’s camp. You can find more information about the waiting list below.

I submitted my application in a long time ago and haven't heard anything back yet. What should I do?

It is possible that your application has not yet been processed. We get hundreds of applications back within a week the opening of the registration site, and process hundreds of applications each year! At the height of the application time for camp, from March 1st until April 15th, we may not process all the applications until about three weeks after the postmark. Take note of the date you sent your application, and if it has been four weeks since you sent it in, please feel free to give us a call. We thank you for your patience.

What band level should I sign up for?

Your band placement will be determined by your audition results. It’s possible for students in ANY grade level to make Symphonic Band, but that group will consist mostly of high school students. Similarly, the Concert Band will consist mainly of students just leaving 6th through 9th grade band.
